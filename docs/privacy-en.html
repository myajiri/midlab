Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

MidLab ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Only on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers:

  • Profile information (age category, gender, athletic experience, personal best records)
  • ETP test results
  • Training plans
  • Workout logs
  • App settings

Data Processed Through Third-Party Services

  • Purchase information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. Purchase transaction information is processed on RevenueCat's servers. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.
  • App Store / Google Play: The App is distributed and billing is processed through Apple and Google services. Please review their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • Storing and displaying training data on your device
  • Processing subscription purchases and restorations

3. Sharing with Third Parties

The App shares purchase information with RevenueCat for subscription management purposes only. We do not share personal information with third parties for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

All training data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App will delete all data from your device.

5. Use by Minors

Minors may use the App under the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian. The App does not require account registration and does not transmit personal information to any server. Subscription purchases should be made by a parent or guardian.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Changes become effective when posted on this page.
