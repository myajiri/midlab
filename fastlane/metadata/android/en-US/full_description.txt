MidLab is a training management app specialized for 800m-1500m middle-distance running.

■ Measure Your Fitness with ETP Tests
Our unique ETP (Endurance Training Pace) test scientifically measures your current fitness level. Based on the results, optimal training paces are automatically calculated.

■ Auto-Generated Training Plans
Based on your ETP test results, weekly training plans tailored to your fitness level are automatically generated. Interval runs, tempo runs, pace runs — all the training varieties essential for middle-distance running.

■ Record & Manage Workouts
Easily record your daily training. Review your workout history to track your training progress.

■ Your Data Stays on Your Device
All training data is stored locally on your device. No account registration required. Start training right away.
